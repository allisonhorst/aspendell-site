EMERGENCY PREPAREDNESS AND INFORMATION

IN CASE OF EMERGENCY, DIAL 9-1-1

Emergency dispatcher will contact the Aspendell South Fork Volunteer Fire Department.

Defibrillator (AED) and oxygen available at the Aspendell fire house.

Sign up for CodeRED public safety alerts:

CodeRED is a mobile app for location-based public safety alerts (e.g. natural disasters, fires, dangerous environmental conditions, etc.). Download the CodeRED Mobile Alert App or create a CodeRED account.

Prepare for Public Safety Power Shutoffs:

From prepareforpowerdown.com: "In order to keep communities safe, your local energy company may need to turn off power during extreme weather or wildfire conditions. This is called a Public Safety Power Shutoff." Visit the site to sign up for alerts from your provider, to learn about ways to prepare for power shutoffs, and for additional information.

About the Aspendell South Fork Volunteer Fire Department:

ASFVFD provides fire protection for the Aspendell and South Fork communities and funding is entirely from voluntary community contributions. Services are rendered without regard to the origin of the contributions received. Membership is voluntary and supporting membership fees are not a consideration for services provided. The ASFVFD service statement is posted here. For more information (volunteers, budget, recent news, etc.) please visit the ASFVFD website.
